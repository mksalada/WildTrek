Our Story

Founded in 2010, WildTrek Youth Adventures began with a simple mission: to connect young people with nature through exciting outdoor experiences.

What started as a small group of outdoor enthusiasts has grown into a thriving community of adventurers. We've guided over 5,000 young people on unforgettable journeys into the wilderness.
