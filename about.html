Our Story

How a passion for adventure became a mission to connect youth with nature

Our Journey

The Beginning

In 2010, our founders Jake and Sarah set out on a simple weekend camping trip with a small group of local youth. What they discovered was a profound need for outdoor experiences that went beyond typical summer camps.

That first trip grew into a monthly adventure club, and by 2015, WildTrek Youth Adventures became a full-fledged non-profit organization serving hundreds of young people each year.

Our Mission

We believe that every young person deserves the opportunity to experience the transformative power of nature. Our mission is to:

  • Provide safe, exciting outdoor adventures for youth ages 12-18
  • Foster leadership skills and self-confidence through wilderness challenges
  • Create lifelong memories and friendships in natural settings
  • Promote environmental stewardship and conservation values
